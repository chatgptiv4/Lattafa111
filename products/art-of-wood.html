Art Of Wood

$49.99 USD

Lattafa Pride Art of Wood Eau de Parfum is a sophisticated unisex fragrance that blends vibrant and earthy elements with refined elegance. It opens with a zesty mix of bergamot, pink pepper, and grapefruit, offering a fresh and invigorating start. The heart reveals a rich blend of rose, patchouli, and cedarwood, adding depth and complexity. The fragrance settles into a warm, grounding base of vetiver, oakmoss, and vanilla, creating a sensual and enduring finish. This scent combines citrus brightness with earthy and woody undertones, making it a versatile and captivating choice for any occasion.

Top Notes
Grapefruit, Bergamot, Pink Pepper

Middle Notes
Cedarwood, Patchouli, Rose

Base Notes
Vanilla, Vetiver, Oakmoss
