Art Of Arabia I

The fragrance opens with a bright and zesty burst of bergamot, a citrus note known for its fresh and invigorating qualities. Paired with the incredible, crisp scent of mint, this initial impression is refreshing and uplifting, setting the stage for the journey ahead. As the fragrance evolves, it transitions into its heart notes, where a symphony of unique and intriguing accords awaits. With its deep and fragrant profile, black tea takes centre stage, creating an aura of warmth and sophistication. Ginger adds a spicy and slightly piquant character, while lavender introduces a touch of herbal freshness. In the base, Art Of Arabia I Perfume reveals its depth and complexity. Ambroxan, a synthetic note reminiscent of ambergris, contributes a musky and sweet facet, enhancing the fragrances longevity and overall sensuality. Olibanum, also known as frankincense, lends a resinous and spiritual quality to the composition, evoking the essence of Arabian culture. With its warm and spicy aroma, Cinnamon adds a final layer of depth and allure. Art Of Arabia I Perfume is a unisex fragrance that transcends gender boundaries. Its multifaceted and intriguing blend of notes makes it suitable for those who appreciate the complexity of their scents. Whether worn during the day or in the evening, this fragrance exudes an air of elegance and refinement. In summary, Art Of Arabia I Perfume pays homage to the beauty and mystique of Arabian traditions. Its harmonious fusion of citrus, tea, spices, and resins creates a captivating and memorable fragrance that celebrates the artistry and allure of Arabia.

Top Notes
Bergamot, Mint

Middle Notes
Black Tea, Ginger, Lavender

Base Notes
Ambroxan, Frankincense, Cinnamon
