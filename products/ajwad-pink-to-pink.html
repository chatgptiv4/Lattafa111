Ajwad Pink To Pink

$39.99 USD

Ajwad Pink to Pink by Lattafa Perfumes is a captivating new fragrance for both women and men, launched in 2024. This enchanting scent opens with a lively and refreshing blend of guava, pink grapefruit, lychee, and pink pepper, creating a vibrant and succulent introduction. As the fragrance develops, the heart notes unveil a beautiful floral bouquet, featuring the timeless elegance of rose, the delicate charm of peony, and the graceful allure of magnolia. In the base, rich and warm notes of leather, musk, ambergris, vanilla, and moss come together, providing a sensual and enduring finish. Ajwad Pink to Pink is a harmonious composition that leaves a lasting impression of sophistication and allure.

Top Notes
Guava, Pink Grapefruit, Litchi, Pink Pepper

Middle Notes
Rose, Peony, Magnolia

Base Notes
Leather, Musk, Ambergris, Vanilla, Moss
